01. About Me

I’m a developer who enjoys turning ideas into real, working systems. With a strong foundation in web development and databases, I like building applications that are not just functional but also practical and user-friendly. From creating full-stack projects like an EV Battery Swap Station Locator to designing task trackers and feedback systems, I focus on learning by building.

I’m particularly interested in how frontend design and backend logic come together to create seamless experiences. While I’m continuously improving my skills in JavaScript and modern web technologies, I already have hands-on experience with Python (Django), MySQL, C/C++, and core web technologies.

Beyond technical work, I actively participate in communities like CodeChef and ACSES, where I grow through collaboration, competitions, and learning from others. These experiences have helped me develop strong teamwork, communication, and leadership skills.

Outside of coding, I enjoy exploring creativity through animation and storytelling projects. I also like unwinding with manga or brainstorming ideas that I can later turn into projects.

I believe in consistent growth — building, improving, and pushing myself step by step toward becoming a better developer.
